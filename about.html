About

I am a media solutions architect working on live broadcast and streaming — distribution, packaging, CDN and transcoding for IPTV and OTT platforms. Self-taught, AWS-certified, and happiest when I can take a protocol apart to see how it really behaves.

This is where I write up the small tools I build and the things I measure along the way. Everything here is hand-written HTML over a single stylesheet — no framework, no tracker, no JavaScript.

Code lives at github.com/benjaminboisseau.
